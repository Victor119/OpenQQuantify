V_am (t) = A_c * cos(2*n*f_c*t)*[1+ K_a * m(t)]
         = A_c * cos(2*n*f_c*t)+[A_c*A_m*K_a*cos(2*n*f_c*t)*cos(2 * n * f_m * t)]


m(t) = modulating signal 
	which is your information signal

m(t) = A_m * cos(2*n*f_m *t)

f_c = frequency

A_c = carrier signal

A_m = amplitude

K_a = Modulation INDEX
